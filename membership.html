FAQ

Common questions.

Do unused notarizations roll over? +
No — unused credits expire at the end of each month. This keeps the service sustainable and priced fairly. If you consistently don't use your allowance, Solo might be more than you need.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
What counts as one notarization? +
One notarization session — regardless of how many documents are signed in that session. A real estate closing with 12 pages counts as one session. A remote online session for a power of attorney counts as one session.
Is the Business plan right for a title company? +
For title companies processing 5–20 closings per month, Business works well. For 20+ closings, Enterprise with net-30 invoicing and a dedicated account manager will serve you better. Contact us and we'll help you choose.
How does invoice billing on Business work? +
Business plan members receive a single monthly invoice instead of per-booking Stripe charges. This eliminates 40+ individual transactions for active B2B clients. Pay one invoice per month, net-15.
Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-month? +
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately (prorated). Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
